CoLab

CoLab is a prototype plugin for Sketch that provides real-time collaborative editing. This was a final project for Mobile Service Innovation at CMU in collaboration with fellow classmates Chris Barker, Rehan Butt, Rishikesh Yardi, and Aparna Sridhar Murthy.

The idea for CoLab was inspired by the difficulties modern designers face in the workplace. Design software such as PhotoShop, Sketch, and others allow for tremendous efficiency and high fidelity. However, the individualistic nature of these tools also hinder what is traditionally a collaborative process.

We prototyped CoLab for Sketch since they offer the most open API's in the design tool space and are rising rapidly in popularity. As part of the class, a large focus was placed on the business viability and logistics of how the product would work. That is, we approached CoLab more as a company rather than a project.
